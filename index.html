HISTORY | CULTURE | COMMUNITY

The Sardargarh Heritage Fort is not just a place but also the home of the royal family with endless stories of the 300 year old history allied to this magnificient battle fort. In line with the family's firm belief to benefit the community, many experiences at the fort have been curated along with the local villagers of Sardargarh.

Overlooking the lake, surrounding by the magnificient Aravalli Hills, the fort is the hidden gem tha has beautifully preserved the medieval architecture an offer guests exceptional experiences like folk music, dance, farm-to-table and much more.
